About

I am a journalist with over 16 years of experience in digital marketing and technology, currently completing the ICT50220 Diploma of Information Technology (Front-End Web Development) at the Australian College of Business Intelligence (ACBI). My studies are focused on building complex, accessible, and standards-compliant web experiences using HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript.

I combine a strong foundation in technology, data analysis, and creative problem-solving to develop user-centred digital products. I am passionate about bridging the gap between design and development — creating websites that are not just visually engaging, but also performant, accessible, and responsive across all devices.

IT Skills & Technical Knowledge

  • Front-End Development: HTML5, CSS3 (external stylesheets, CSS variables, Flexbox, Grid), JavaScript (DOM manipulation, sessionStorage)
  • Responsive Web Design: Mobile-first layouts, media queries, cross-device testing
  • Web Standards & Accessibility: W3C compliance, WCAG 2.1, WAVE accessibility validation
  • CSS Validation: W3C CSS Validator, debugging and resolving stylesheet errors
  • Cross-Browser Testing: BrowserStack (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari), device simulation
  • Version Control & Hosting: GitHub, GitHub Pages deployment
  • Web Security: Cyber security best practices applicable to web development
  • Website Development: Full website builds including e-commerce layouts, registration forms, and interactive features
  • Data Analysis & Technology: Integrating data and analytics tools into digital marketing and business strategies

Current Project

As part of my diploma assessment (ICTWEB519 & ICTWEB520), I developed the Computer Force website — a multi-page e-commerce platform for an online electronics retailer based in Parramatta, Sydney. The project demonstrates skills in complex page layouts, responsive design, external CSS architecture, interactive shopping cart functionality, and full cross-browser and accessibility validation.

Professional Background

Beyond IT, I bring extensive experience in digital marketing, social media strategy, content creation, copywriting, and business consulting. I have worked with startups and established companies across Brazil and Australia, including mentoring businesses in acceleration programs and developing storytelling-driven marketing strategies. This cross-disciplinary background gives me a unique perspective when building digital solutions that must connect with real users.
